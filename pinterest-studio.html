HK23 · PINTEREST STUDIO

30 ready-to-post pins (10 artworks × 3 angles). Each pin targets a different buyer — gallery, cozy, minimalist. Pinterest is the #1 driver for printable art and pins keep working for months. Use the matching mockup PNG as the pin image.

⚡ Quick Pinterest playbook

  1. Make a free Pinterest Business account → enables analytics + Rich Pins.
  2. Create boards by theme: “Surreal Wall Art”, “Cozy Home Prints”, “Cyberpunk & Neon”, “Woodland / Nature Art”, “Psychedelic Art”.
  3. Pin image = the mockup (framed-on-wall converts far better than raw art).
  4. Link every pin to its exact Etsy listing (not just the shop) — set the URL below.
  5. Post 3–5 pins/day, spread across boards. Re-pin the same art in different styles on different days.
  6. First sales/reviews take ~2–4 weeks of consistent pinning — it compounds.
