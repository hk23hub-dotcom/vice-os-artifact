01 — Vision
The Problem
We're Solving

Amateur golfers in Chile train without data, without structure, and without accountability. 90% of strokes are lost inside 100 yards, yet training happens at the range hitting drivers. There is no premium, data-integrated performance facility between Santiago and La Serena.

The Opportunity
Las Rocas Golf Club has the location, the member base, and the land. ViceGolfer has the digital platform, the data model, and the training methodology. What's missing is the physical infrastructure.
The Vision
A physical space where every session is tracked, every putt is logged, and improvement is measured — not felt. Integrated with the ViceGolfer app from day one. Premium, intentional, limited.

03 — Physical Spaces
The Four Zones
Each zone has distinct spatial, structural, and technical requirements. The architect's brief covers all four.
Zone 01
Golftainer
Full-Swing Simulator Pod
12m × 3m × 3m H
Shipping Container
A converted high-cube shipping container housing a professional golf simulator. Launch monitor data. Video analysis. The ability to play any course in the world. Standalone module — can be relocated, expanded, or replicated.
STRUCTURAL REQUIREMENTS
High-cube container (9'6" height) — minimum 3m clear ceiling
Reinforced floor for simulator platform + hitting mat (approx. 2×2m)
Blackout: no ambient light. Full blackout interior
Climate control: HVAC unit, 18–22°C maintained
Vibration dampening: rubber floor underlayer
3-phase power supply: minimum 20A circuit
Projector mount point: ceiling reinforced at 2.8m+ height
TECHNOLOGY INSIDE
Launch monitor: Trackman 4 or Foresight GCQuad
4K short-throw projector (3.5m throw distance)
Impact screen: 3m × 2.5m — premium enclosure net
Side cameras: 2× for swing video capture
Simulator software: E6 Connect or GSPro
Display monitor: 55" side for data output
ViceGolfer API integration: auto-log sessions
Zone 02
Putting Lab
The Protocol Room — 100 Putts Daily
8m × 6m
Dedicated Room
The heart of the operation. The ViceGolfer Putting Protocol — 100 putts per day, 2ft to 6ft, logged and analyzed — lives here. Purpose-built surface. SAM PuttLab integration. The room where handicaps drop.
STRUCTURAL REQUIREMENTS
Minimum 8m straight run for lag putting (15–25ft)
6m wide to allow 3 parallel station setup
Level floor: tolerance ±1mm over 6m (critical for putting)
Climate control: humidity 45–55%, temperature constant
Diffused lighting: no shadows on putting surface. LED panels
Acoustic treatment: low ambient noise
Minimal 2.6m ceiling clearance
TECHNOLOGY INSIDE
SAM PuttLab (Science & Motion) — stroke analysis
Premium artificial putting surface — Stimpmeter 10–12
Distance markers: 2ft, 3ft, 4ft, 5ft, 6ft, 10ft, 15ft, 20ft, 25ft
Overhead camera + tripod points for video capture
iPad mount: ViceGolfer app for live logging
Wall display: 32" for real-time stats output
Zone 03
Short Game Zone
Chipping · Bunker · Wedge Dial
Outdoor / Semi-covered
400m² target area
Everything inside 100 yards. Chipping stations with target zones, a practice bunker, and a wedge distance dial protocol. Where the scoring shots are built.
SPATIAL REQUIREMENTS
Minimum 80m carry distance for wedge work (50–80 yard shots)
3–4 chipping stations, surface: natural grass or artificial
1 practice bunker: minimum 4×3m, depth 60cm, tour-quality sand
Target areas: raised rings at 10m, 20m, 30m, 50m, 80m
Covered hitting area: minimum 4m canopy for rain protection
Lighting: LED spots for evening sessions
TECHNOLOGY & EQUIPMENT
Trackman portable or FlightScope radar
Distance markers at each target zone
ViceGolfer app: chipping session log
Shot zones for SVG dispersion mapping (in-app)
Outdoor display stand for wind/data output
Zone 04
Clubhouse
Operations · Strategy · Community
60–80m²
Hub Structure
The operations and community hub. Where members review sessions, plan training, and connect. Reception, lounge, analysis station, and admin. The face of the Performance House.
SPATIAL REQUIREMENTS
Reception / check-in desk: visible from entrance
Members lounge: 4–6 seating positions, low tables
Analysis station: 2 desks, wall-mounted 55" display
Equipment storage: locker area for 20 members
Bathroom: 1 WC + shower minimum
Brand: dark walls, gold accents. ViceGolfer palette.
TECHNOLOGY & OPERATIONS
Central admin: check-in, scheduling, member management
Wall display: live leaderboard + member stats dashboard
ViceGolfer admin panel: full app access on tablet
Content creation corner: tripod point + ring light mount
WiFi: dedicated fiber line, mesh throughout all zones

For the architect: the spatial concept is a connected campus, not a single building. The Clubhouse + Putting Lab form one indoor structure. The Golftainer is a standalone module adjacent. The Short Game Zone is semi-covered outdoor space. The challenge is circulation — members should flow naturally between zones without friction.
