01 — What This Is
Read This First
This is NOT
  • A golf simulator business
  • A golf academy
  • A public facility
  • A driving range
  • A commercial clubhouse
  • A pay-per-session operation
This IS
  • The founder's private residence
  • A performance + creation headquarters
  • A private members ecosystem
  • A content production machine
  • A living proof of concept
  • The first node of Vice OS

Think: Performance House. Think: Creator House. Think: Startup Garage. Think: Fraternity of Performance.

The house is where the founder lives, trains, creates, and documents. Founding members come here to participate in the system — not as customers buying a service, but as part of the experiment. Every session generates data. Every improvement generates content. Every member becomes a case study.

The Real Product
The ViceGolfer Method. A repeatable system for measurable golf improvement, documented in real time through data, video, and performance analytics.
The Real Asset
Data + Results + Documentation. Members who improve. Proof that the system works. Content that reaches thousands. A platform built on real outcomes.

System 02 · THE CROWN JEWEL
Putting Lab
Indoor · Controlled · Data-Mandatory
HARDEST
TO REPLICATE
This is NOT a practice green. This is a controlled performance laboratory where putters are built through structure and data. Reservation required. Sessions logged. Progress tracked. Video captured. No exceptions.
What happens here
100 Putt Protocol — daily tracked sessions
Distance control testing (2ft → 25ft)
Pressure challenges — scored, benchmarked
PuttView projection system (lines, break, targets)
Video capture — every session
Spatial requirements
Min. 8m straight run for lag putting
Level floor tolerance ±1mm — critical
Blackout capable — no ambient light
Climate control — humidity 45–55%
Diffused LED — no shadows on surface
PuttView + iPad station
System 03 · Community + Content
Outdoor Putting Green
Open use · Social · Events · Content
NOT the same as the Putting Lab

The Outdoor Green attracts people. The Indoor Lab transforms people. These serve completely different purposes. Never design or describe them as the same system.

The social and content hub of the house. Open, inviting, competitive. Where challenges happen, where cameras roll, where community forms.
What happens here
Open challenges + friendly competitions
Live content creation — TikTok, Reels
Events + networking sessions
Intro sessions for new members
Spatial concept
Outdoor, natural light, photogenic
Multiple hole targets + flagsticks
Seating around perimeter — social design
Camera-friendly angles — brand backdrop

05 — Member Model
20 Founding
Participants
These are not customers. They are founding participants who help validate the system. Their results become the proof of concept.

Frame shift: A "member" is someone who signs up and pays for access. A "founding participant" is someone who joins the experiment — they get direct access to the founder, contribute to the data set, and their improvement builds the case for everything that comes next.

What They Get

Full performance data tracking
Personal performance profile in app

Community + house access
Documentation of their progress
What They Give

Data logged — every session, no exceptions
Content participation (not required, valued)
Feedback on the system
Investment in their own improvement

07 — For You
What We're
Building Together
You're not designing a commercial facility. You're designing a private performance and creation headquarters that happens to be someone's home.

The design challenge: make a private home feel like a high-performance environment — without losing the intimacy that makes it work. A golf academy feels institutional. ViceGolfer House should feel like you've been invited somewhere special.

01
Putting Lab First
This is the highest priority build. Get this right first. Precision floor, controlled environment, tech integration. See full specs in System 02.
02
Content Studio
Second priority. Acoustic treatment, lighting design, camera-ready angles. The studio is the business engine — it needs to be professional.
03
Flow + Privacy
Members should not access the founder's private spaces by default. Design the flow so the Putting Lab and Content Studio are accessible without penetrating the private areas.
04
Outdoor Design for Camera
The outdoor putting green and gym are content production spaces as much as training spaces. Design for camera angles, natural light, and brand aesthetics.
05
Long Game
This is the first physical Vice OS node. The architect who builds this becomes the design partner for all future nodes — RugbyVice, Golfteiner deployments, additional houses. Think in systems.
